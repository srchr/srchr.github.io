About Us

Since 1988, the Schnitzelhaus has been one of the leading authentic Bavarian restaurants in Victoria. The Schnitzelhaus brought a much needed Bavarian flavour to the area which has been appreciated by our great customers.
Now, almost twenty years later, the citizens of Melbourne know that we are the number one authentic Bavarian restaurant in town. Schnitzelhaus wants its customers to let themselves free of all stress and have a good time with the best bier, the best schnitzel, the best music, and of course the best people.
